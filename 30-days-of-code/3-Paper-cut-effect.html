PAPER CUT-OUT EFFECT

Each letter is made out of a base and a top. So, we have two letters stacked on top of each other. The top letter or layer is skewed along the Y-axis to give it the 'paper cut-out effect' and given a shadow. On hover, animation is added which changes the skewing degree along the Y-axis and the respective position of their shadows
